## FAQ

### Sub-Headline
Frequently asked questions

### Headline
Questions? We have answers

### How is Glimmer different from newsletters, RSS or ChatGPT?

Most tools help find information reactively.

Glimmer is designed for something different.

Detect critical market signals, interpret them in the context of your company and convert them into actionable decisions inside the team.

It is not a tool for information consumption or search.

It is a proactive decision infrastructure adapted to your company context.

### How does Glimmer personalise signals for each company?

Glimmer adapts to the specific context of each organisation.

During the initial setup we define the company market environment including industry, key markets, competitors, relevant clients and strategic priorities.

From there the system automatically detects and prioritises signals that can actually impact your business.

### Who typically uses Glimmer inside a company?

Glimmer is designed for profiles with strategic responsibility or direct exposure to the market.

- Executive leadership
- Strategy and business development
- Innovation and transformation
- Commercial teams
- Marketing and market analysis
- Operations

The goal is for decision makers to have direct access to the signals that can impact their area.

### How does Glimmer ensure information quality?

Glimmer continuously analyses relevant open sources and uses artificial intelligence models to detect signals that can truly have an impact.

Each signal is structured with context, potential impact and clear recommendations to facilitate team evaluation.

The objective is not to show more information but to filter noise and highlight only what can change decisions.

### How much time does my team need to use Glimmer?

Glimmer reduces up to 90% of the time spent in traditional manual research processes.

Signals appear already interpreted and contextualised so teams can focus directly on evaluating impact and making decisions.

### How long does implementation take?

Glimmer is not a plug and play tool.

To ensure the system detects the signals that truly matter we first perform a setup process to understand the business, the areas to monitor and the desired coverage level.

Technology and AI help accelerate the process while ensuring rigorous configuration.

Average implementation time is around one month for mid sized companies and between one and three months for enterprise organisations.

### What pricing model does the platform have?

Glimmer offers different subscription plans that scale mainly according to:

- Signal coverage
- Number of users
- Integration level

This allows companies to start with a specific scope and expand progressively as the system generates value for more teams.

To receive a proposal adapted to your context you can request a demo.

### What level of support does Glimmer provide?

During implementation we work closely with each client to adapt the system to their context and ensure the detected signals are relevant.

Afterwards we provide continuous support to adjust signal coverage, incorporate new strategic areas and optimise adoption inside the team.

Our goal is for Glimmer to become a stable layer of market intelligence within the organisation.
